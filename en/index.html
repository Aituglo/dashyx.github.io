What is Dashyx ?
Dashyx is an application that allows to group all these web applications in one place. No need to download full application on your PC when only one is enough.

Support | FAQ

Dashyx is free ?

Yes. Dashyx is a completely free application. You can add as many apps as you want, for free. Work and productivity should be free!
Does Dashyx keep my credentials?

No. Dashyx does not remember your login information for your applications on our servers. They are stored in the same way as in a browser, in your cookies.
Why did I create Dashyx?

I needed to have all my apps handy, and that without needing to change page all the time. So I created Dashyx to be faster and more productive.
What is the difference between Dashyx and a web browser ?

This has nothing to do with a browser, because here you group everything in the same place, and even your desktop applications to enable better productivity and a new way of working.
